About TReLiSS


Transparency in the Research Lifecycle in the Social Sciences (TReLiSS) is a research coordination network focusing on reproducibility in the quantitative social sciences, with a Steering Committee composed of economists, political scientists, sociologists, but who also identify as demographers, policy analysts, data scientists, and statisticians.

The network is not yet active, but have submitted a funding request to the National Science Foundation.

This page lists some of the projects that the network will undertake, subject to availability of funding.
